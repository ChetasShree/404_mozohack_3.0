About Us

Team 404 is a group of people who were just paranoid about the rising privacy concerns and hidden data collection and scary things like that by all these powerful social media sites, not to mention a very toxic and non inclusive environment building up on there. Hence, we decided to create a safe space for us and everyone else who feels their data MUST ONLY be theirs. This was the birth story of Babbler!
